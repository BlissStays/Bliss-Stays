About this property

Located directly across from the picturesque Kennington Park, this beautifully designed four-bedroom apartment offers the perfect balance of comfort, style, and convenience. Whether you're visiting with family, friends, or colleagues, the space is thoughtfully furnished and fully equipped to meet all your needs.

Each bedroom features a comfortable double bed with crisp linens, creating a restful haven after a busy day. The open-plan living area provides ample seating for relaxation, whether you're unwinding with a movie or sharing time with your group.

The kitchen is fully stocked with everything you need to prepare meals at home—including a stove, oven, microwave, kettle, and all essential cookware. Enjoy your meals together in the adjacent dining area, designed for easy and sociable dining.

You’ll also benefit from fast Wi-Fi, a Smart TV for entertainment, and central heating to keep you cosy throughout your stay. Self check-in adds an extra layer of convenience, allowing for flexible arrivals.

The apartment is ideally located between two major tube stations—Oval and Vauxhall—providing quick access to Central London. Enjoy the peace of a residential area with the city just minutes away. Across the street, Kennington Park offers a beautiful green space for morning jogs, strolls, or casual picnics. You'll also be surrounded by fantastic local eateries, cafés, and pubs that give you a real taste of London living.
